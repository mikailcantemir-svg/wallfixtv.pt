Frequently asked questions about TV wall mounting

Can I mount a TV on a plasterboard wall?

Yes, but the wall structure must be assessed and suitable fixings used. Not all plasterboard walls support the same weight, so installation must be done carefully.

Is the TV bracket included?

We can install a bracket supplied by the customer or advise on the most suitable type for the TV and wall.

Do you also hide cables?

Yes, we can organise or conceal cables depending on the wall type and desired solution.

How long does the installation take?

In most cases, installation takes between 1 and 2 hours, depending on wall type, TV size and bracket used.

Do you mount large TVs?

Yes, we mount TVs of various sizes. For large televisions, we assess weight, bracket and wall type before installation.
